FAQ

Questions we
hear all the time.

Everything you need to know before your visit. Still have questions? Chat with Aivy or give us a call — we're happy to help.

Do you take walk-ins? +
Yes! Walk-ins are always welcome at Red Persimmon. However, booking ahead guarantees your preferred technician and time slot. For busy periods like weekends, we recommend booking in advance to avoid wait times.
Do you offer gift certificates? +
Yes! Gift certificates are available in any amount and never expire. They make a perfect gift for birthdays, holidays, Mother's Day, or any special occasion. You can purchase them in store or by calling us at (603) 621-7469.
How long does a full set take? +
A full set typically takes 60–90 minutes depending on the style and complexity. Simple sets take closer to an hour, while detailed nail art or ombre sets may take longer. We recommend booking a 90-minute slot for your first visit.
How long do lash extensions last? +
Lash extensions typically last 2–3 weeks before needing a fill, depending on your natural lash growth cycle and how well you care for them. We recommend fills every 2 weeks to keep them looking full and beautiful.
Can I request a specific technician? +
Absolutely! You can book directly with your preferred technician using their individual booking links on this page. You can also ask Aivy our AI assistant to help match you with the right technician for your service.
Is there parking available? +
Yes! Free parking is available right at our location — 1500 S. Willow Street, across from American Eagle and close to the Food Court. We're easy to find!
What is your cancellation policy? +
We appreciate as much notice as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule. For groups of 6 or more, we ask for at least 24 hours notice. For individual appointments, please try to give us a heads up so we can offer the slot to another client.
Do you do children's nails? +
Yes! We offer children's manicures ($20) and pedicures ($35) for kids aged 10 and under. It's a wonderful experience for mother-daughter outings or special occasions.
